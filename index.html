Settings

AL/MC entitlement is auto-calculated by staff Work Location, Confirmation Date and Termination/Resignation Date. Local: AL 14→21, MC 14. Remote: AL 10→18, MC 10. AL increases by 1 day on the first day of each year and is prorated for confirmation/termination year.

Public Holiday Settings

Admin can add future Singapore Public Holidays here when MOM releases the new year dates. These dates will be used for calendar display and leave working-day calculation.

AL Manual Adjustment

Administrator can manually add or deduct AL balance for yearly forfeiture, carry forward, special approval, or correction without changing the entitlement engine.
